## 3D Game Development
When it comes to 3D game development, I want to show off a platformer type game I made.
It takes the player through a facility type area as they escape to the outside.
For the models and animations, I used free assets from the internet.
All the scripts and level design was made by me.
I liked how the level felt challenging, especially in the final jump, to those who playtested it.
I didn't like how the atmosphere felt and that I could have made the industrial atmosphere come across stronger.
