## 3D Game Development
When it comes to 3D game development, I want to show off a platformer type game I made.
It takes the player through a facility type area as they escape to the outside.

For the models and animations, I used free assets from the internet.
All the scripts and level design was made by me.

I liked how the level felt challenging, especially in the final jump, to those who playtested it.
I didn't like how the atmosphere felt and that I could have made the industrial atmosphere come across stronger.
For future projects I'll try to spend more time polishing the atmosphere and making it feel better.

## 2D Game Development
For this I'll show off another platforming game I developed on my own.
In this game you play as a knight that traverses through a dangerous level avoiding enemies and pitfalls.

I made all the models, animations and scripts myself along with the level design.
The quality of these assets vary due to my inexperience with art.

I liked how the level design turned out, with the level offering different challenges throughout and requiring precise inputs to avoid.
I didn't like how some features needed to be cut, such as player attacks, due to time constraints.
This taught me to be aware of remaining time and pace myself so I can get the features I want done with good time left for future projects.
