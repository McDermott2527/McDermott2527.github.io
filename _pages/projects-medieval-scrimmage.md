Medieval Scrimmage is the most recent game development project that I've been working on.

The game is a 2D Beat 'Em Up style game.
It's about a peasant who goes on a quest to defeat an evil king to avenge the execution of his wife.

The game features multiple waves and enemy types, many player weapons and pickups and bosses.
So far on the game, I've contributed UI features, combo and projectile systems, bug fixes and much more.
